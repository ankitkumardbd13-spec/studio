# **App Name**: MPITI Saharanpur Connect

## Core Features:

- Public Institute Portal: A public-facing homepage displaying ITI information, courses, faculty messages, location, contact details, and student testimonials.
- Student Registration & Profile Management: Students can sign up with personal and educational details. Admin reviews and approves new registrations, and students can view their complete profile after approval.
- Secure Student Dashboard: Logged-in students get a personalized welcome with their name and photo, access to assignments, notifications, trade-specific syllabus, results, and fee payment options.
- Digital ITI ID Card Generator: Students can digitally generate and view their ITI ID card, complete with institute logo, seal, photo, personal details, trade, roll number, and session validity.
- Online Admission Form: Provide an accessible online admission form for prospective students, with administrative tools to access, review, and print submitted applications.
- Admin Control Panel: An administrator panel to manage all public content (ITI details, messages), approve/reject student sign-ups, download student data, manage assignments, and update course syllabuses.
- AI-Powered Assignment & Mock Test Creator Tool: An AI tool for administrators to assist in drafting assignment questions and creating mock test links based on specific trade syllabuses or topics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Light color scheme. Primary color: a rich, earthy gold (#B88E3E) to convey tradition and excellence. Background color: a desaturated, light cream (#FAFAF0) for clarity and readability. Accent color: a vibrant, warm orange-red (#EC6947) to highlight calls to action and important elements.
- Headline font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for an elegant, intellectual, and traditional feel. Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for modern clarity and excellent readability.
- Use clear, professional icons that are easy to understand, reflecting educational and administrative themes without being overly playful or abstract.
- Clean and organized layouts with clear sectioning. A prominent sidebar for navigation and login options, and content areas that are easy to scan and read, particularly for course details and student profiles.
- Subtle and functional animations to indicate user interactions, such as form submissions, menu expansions, or loading states, enhancing user experience without distracting.
